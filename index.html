We focus on issues that unlock progress on gender equality.

UN Women is a global champion for women and girls. We work to ensure that they have the same rights and opportunities as men and boys, and that they are able to fully participate in all spheres of life. We strive to eliminate all forms of discrimination against women and girls, and work to empower them to take control of their own lives and contribute to the development of their communities and countries.

Our Impact

UN Women has made progress in advancing gender equality and the empowerment of women and girls around the world.

Supporting Women's Economic Empowerment

UN Women supports programs and initiatives that help women gain access to economic opportunities, including education and training, financial services, and property rights.

Combating Violence Against Women

UN Women works to prevent and respond to violence against women and girls, including through the development of laws, policies, and programs that support survivors and hold perpetrators accountable.

Promoting Gender Equality

UN Women promotes gender equality and the empowerment of women by advocating for the adoption and the implementation of international agreements that support these goals.

Advancing Women's Political Participation

UN Women supports efforts to increase women's participation in political and public life, including through initiatives to promote women's leadership and representation in decision-making roles.

A Brief History

UN Women is the United Nations agency for gender equality and the empowerment of women. It was established in 2011 through the merger of four previously existing UN agencies, and has a mandate to focus specifically on these issues. UN Women works to promote gender equality and the empowerment of women globally through policy advocacy, research, and partnerships. Its key areas of focus include ending violence against women and girls, increasing women's participation in political and economic decision-making, and promoting women's rights in the context of peace and security. The organization also supports the implementation of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) in relation to gender equality and women's empowerment.
